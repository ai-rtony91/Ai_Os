# AI_OS Pricing And Packaging Draft

## Purpose

This is a pricing and packaging placeholder only. It does not approve final pricing, billing, subscriptions, payment processing, app-store billing, or entitlement enforcement.

## Possible Future Tiers

- Free
- Pro
- Advanced
- Enterprise

## Feature Gating Placeholder

Future feature gating may separate local dashboard features, telemetry previews, advanced analytics, reporting, AI usage, and enterprise governance. No feature gate is approved here.

## Telemetry Usage Boundary

Pricing must not depend on telemetry collection until privacy policy, consent, retention, deletion/export, and compliance placeholders mature.

## AI Usage Cost Boundary

Future AI usage pricing must account for model costs, local-first constraints, provider disclosure, rate limits, and user consent.

## Cloud Cost Boundary

Future cloud cost planning must identify hosting, storage, analytics, support, and compliance costs before public launch.

## Non-Approval Statement

This draft does not approve final pricing, subscriptions, billing provider setup, app-store billing, analytics SDKs, or user billing collection.
