# AI_OS Business Telemetry Privacy Boundary Draft

## Purpose

This draft defines privacy and compliance constraints for future business telemetry. It is documentation only.

## Allowed Purpose

Future business telemetry may support product planning, packaging, licensing readiness, support demand review, and compliance gate tracking.

## Required Controls

- Legal/compliance review before collection.
- Explicit consent model before persistence.
- Payment-data exclusion.
- Broker-data exclusion.
- Private-data exclusion.
- Credential exclusion.
- Retention and deletion rules before writing files.

## Blocked Actions

- No telemetry writer.
- No telemetry collector.
- No monetization launch.
- No billing integration.
- No app-store analytics.
- No remote analytics.
- No persistence.
- No broker/API access.

## Unknowns

- UNKNOWN: pricing model.
- UNKNOWN: billing provider.
- UNKNOWN: app-store distribution path.
- UNKNOWN: legal retention requirements.
- UNKNOWN: privacy policy language.
