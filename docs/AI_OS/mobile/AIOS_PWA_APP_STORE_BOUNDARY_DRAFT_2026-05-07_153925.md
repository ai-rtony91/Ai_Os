# AI_OS PWA App Store Boundary Draft

## Purpose

This draft defines planning boundaries for future PWA and app-store review. It is documentation only.

## PWA vs Native App Distinction

A PWA may use web distribution concepts. A native app may require store packaging, signing, platform review, and app-store policy compliance. Neither path is approved by this draft.

## Google Play Placeholder Concerns

Future Google Play review may require privacy policy, data safety disclosures, account deletion process, subscription disclosures, AI disclosures, support contact, and financial/trading disclaimers.

## Apple App Store Placeholder Concerns

Future Apple review may require privacy nutrition labels, terms, subscription disclosures, support contact, account deletion process, AI disclosures, and financial/trading disclaimers.

## Privacy Policy Requirement Placeholder

A privacy policy placeholder must mature before telemetry collection, analytics, app publishing, account systems, or monetization.

## Terms Requirement Placeholder

Terms placeholder language must mature before public distribution, accounts, subscriptions, or paid tiers.

## Trading Disclaimer Placeholder

Any trading-related dashboard, Trading Lab, broker boundary, or OANDA reference requires a future trading disclaimer review.

## Telemetry Disclosure Placeholder

Telemetry disclosure must explain user telemetry, app telemetry, business telemetry, retention, deletion/export, and blocked data classes before collection.

## Non-Approval Statements

This draft does not approve app submission, payment or subscription activation, production legal claims, service-worker registration, analytics SDKs, API calls, broker code, credentials, or trading execution.
