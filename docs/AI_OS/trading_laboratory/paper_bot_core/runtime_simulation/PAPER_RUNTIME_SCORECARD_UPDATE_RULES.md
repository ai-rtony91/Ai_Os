# Paper Runtime Scorecard Update Rules

The Paper Scorecard is for paper review only. It must not claim profitability, live readiness, or real trading approval.

Required Paper Scorecard fields:

- trade_count
- win_rate
- average_win
- average_loss
- expectancy
- profit_factor
- max_drawdown
- confidence_score
- blocked_reason
- review_status

If no Paper Trade Result exists, do not inflate results.

If the Paper Risk Gate blocks, keep metrics neutral and explain the blocked reason.
