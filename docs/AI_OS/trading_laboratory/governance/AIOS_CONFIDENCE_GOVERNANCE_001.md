# AI_OS Confidence Governance 001

Confidence is governed evidence, not optimism.

## Rules

- Confidence rises slowly and falls quickly.
- Strategies must continuously re-prove edge.
- Rolling stability required.
- Replay testing required before trust increases.
- Edge validation required before confidence increases.
- Reject unstable strategies.

## Confidence Hysteresis

Confidence hysteresis means AI_OS can reduce confidence quickly when instability appears, but must require more evidence before confidence rises again.

## Trust Gates

Trust requires paper evidence, stable regimes, controlled drawdown pressure, reliable sessions, portfolio survivability, and repeated replay validation.

## Safety

Confidence governance does not permit live execution. Confidence can affect recommendations only.
