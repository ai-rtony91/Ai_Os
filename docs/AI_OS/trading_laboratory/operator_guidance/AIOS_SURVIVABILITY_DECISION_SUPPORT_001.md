# AI_OS Survivability Decision Support 001

The decision-support layer keeps the operator calm and defensive during unstable paper-only conditions.

## UX Philosophy

- Guidance without pressure.
- Explainability before authority.
- Survivability-first recommendations.
- Calm operator workflow.
- Low cognitive overload.
- Defensive guidance behavior.
- No hype language.
- No execution urgency.

## Boundaries

AI_OS may explain, recommend defensive behavior, and constrain next safe actions. It may not execute, route orders, connect to brokers, or become hidden decision authority.

## Paper-Only Status

Live execution, broker APIs, execution controls, autonomous controls, hidden live routing, and network order routing remain BLOCKED.
