# AI_OS Edge Proof Report 001

## Edge Proof Summary

The proof-of-edge laboratory requires measurable paper evidence before AI_OS can trust a strategy. A strategy must prove rolling expectancy, rolling stability, drawdown survivability, replay survivability, regime reliability, portfolio survivability, and confidence freeze compliance.

## Edge Decay Findings

Edge decay is detected when rolling expectancy weakens, replay survivability fails, drawdown pressure rises, or regime reliability deteriorates.

## Replay Verification Summary

Replay survival is required before promotion. A strategy that cannot survive replay stress remains UNVERIFIED, PARTIALLY_VERIFIED, RECOVERY_REQUIRED, or CONFIDENCE_RESTRICTED.

## Confidence Restriction Summary

Confidence cannot increase without evidence. Minimum sample size, rolling stability, regime reliability, survivability audit, and acceptable drawdown pressure are required.

## Unstable Edge Suppression Summary

Unstable edge must be distrusted. AI_OS suppresses ranking and blocks promotion when edge decay, fake recovery, or unstable regime behavior appears.

## Governance Philosophy

Prove edge before trusting edge. Survivability over aggressive profit. Confidence must be earned slowly. Skepticism remains permanent.
