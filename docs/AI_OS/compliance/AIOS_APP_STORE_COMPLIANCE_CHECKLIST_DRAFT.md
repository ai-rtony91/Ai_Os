# AI_OS App Store Compliance Checklist Draft

## Purpose

This is a placeholder checklist for future Google Play and Apple App Store review. It is not legal advice and does not approve app submission.

## Checklist

- Privacy policy required.
- Terms required.
- Telemetry disclosure required.
- Subscription disclosure required if monetized.
- AI disclosure placeholder required.
- Financial/trading disclaimer placeholder required.
- Account deletion process placeholder required if accounts exist.
- Support contact placeholder required.
- Data retention disclosure required.
- Data export/deletion process required.
- App permissions review required.
- Accessibility review required.
- Security review required.

## Google Play Placeholder

Future Google Play review may require data safety form alignment, account deletion disclosure, subscription disclosure, and support contact.

## Apple App Store Placeholder

Future Apple review may require privacy labels, account deletion disclosure, subscription disclosure, AI/trading disclaimers, and support contact.

## Non-Approval Statement

This checklist does not approve app submission, service-worker registration, production publishing, analytics SDKs, payment integration, broker integration, or trading execution.
