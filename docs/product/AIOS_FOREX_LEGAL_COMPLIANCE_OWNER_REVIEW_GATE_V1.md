# AIOS Forex Legal Compliance Owner Review Gate V1

## Status

Internal product policy. This document is not legal advice, not financial-services advice, not a privacy policy, not a user agreement, and not release approval.

AIOS Forex is not Play Store ready until final release review. AIOS Forex is not legally or commercially approved until owner legal/compliance review.

## Purpose

This gate defines owner review requirements before AIOS Forex is publicly released, sold, submitted to an app store, or presented with financial-services claims.

## Not Legal Advice

This document is an internal checklist and blocker map. It does not determine legal duties, satisfy regulatory obligations, approve commercial launch, or replace attorney/compliance review.

## Owner And Legal Review Required

Owner review and qualified legal/compliance review are required before:

- public launch.
- sale.
- app-store submission.
- paid promotion.
- financial-services claims.
- investment-advice claims.
- user agreement publication.
- privacy policy publication.
- live broker execution.
- third-party SDK or analytics activation.

## Financial-Services Review Required

AIOS Forex must be reviewed for financial-services risk before release. Review must cover product classification, permitted claims, risk disclosures, trading authority, user consent, jurisdiction boundaries, and broker-term constraints.

## Jurisdiction Review Required

Jurisdiction review is required before release or sale. AIOS Forex must not assume that one jurisdiction's review clears another jurisdiction.

## Broker Terms Review Required

Broker terms review is required before any broker-facing public claim, live execution path, evidence publication, or integration release. Broker receipts and evidence must remain sanitized.

## Privacy And Data-Safety Review Required

Privacy/data-safety review must approve:

- data classes.
- forbidden data classes.
- consent language.
- local evidence handling.
- third-party services.
- retention.
- deletion/export.
- support-data handling.
- store data-safety declarations.

## Store Policy Review Required

Store policy review must cover financial-risk disclosures, permission disclosures, data-safety declarations, screenshots, videos, descriptions, user support, account deletion/export if applicable, and background execution behavior.

## User Agreement Required

A user agreement is required before sale or public launch. It must be owner/legal reviewed and must not be generated or treated as final by Codex.

## Privacy Policy Required

A privacy policy is required before sale, public launch, or store submission. It must be owner/legal reviewed and must match actual product behavior.

## Support And Escalation Required

Before release, AIOS Forex must define:

- user support channel.
- escalation path.
- emergency stop path.
- shutdown path.
- refund or commercial-support policy if sold.
- incident evidence handling.
- owner escalation boundary.
