# AI_OS Early Warning Report 001

## Summary

This report describes how AI_OS detects paper-only instability before it becomes severe.

## Detected Warning Triggers

- Rolling stability degradation.
- Rapid drawdown expansion.
- Fake confidence recovery.
- Unstable edge transitions.
- Volatility spikes.
- Correlation overload.
- Portfolio stress concentration.
- Repeated weak-edge behavior.

## Fake Recovery Detection

Fake recovery means confidence appears to improve before enough paper evidence proves recovery. AI_OS blocks fast re-promotion and requires additional paper sessions before confidence can increase again.

## Drawdown Escalation

Drawdown pressure moves warnings from CAUTION to WATCH, UNSTABLE, CRITICAL, or LOCKDOWN depending on severity and portfolio concentration.

## Confidence Freeze Activity

When instability appears, confidence increases are frozen. Confidence may still decrease. Ranking priority and simulated paper size are reduced.

## Lockdown Activation Summary

LOCKDOWN is triggered only during severe paper-only instability, such as portfolio overload, extreme drawdown pressure, or high correlation risk.

## Safety

This report is paper-only. Live execution, broker APIs, network execution, and autonomous execution remain BLOCKED.
