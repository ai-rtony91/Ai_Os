# AI_OS Adaptive Operator Guidance 001

Stage 16.4 creates the first adaptive operator-guidance layer for AI_OS Trading Lab.

This is operator guidance only. It is not autonomous execution, automated trade placement, broker routing, hidden AI decision authority, or network order routing.

## Purpose

AI_OS guidance helps the operator understand constrained actions during instability. It explains why conditions are distrusted, why confidence is restricted, and why defensive behavior is recommended.

## Required Guidance States

- NORMAL
- CAUTION
- WATCH
- UNSTABLE
- LOCKDOWN
- CONFIDENCE_FROZEN
- RECOVERY_REQUIRED
- ELEVATED_RISK

## Required Guidance Explanations

- Why confidence is restricted.
- Why replay failed.
- Why ranking decreased.
- Why portfolio exposure reduced.
- Why WATCH activated.
- Why LOCKDOWN activated.
- Why Next Safe Action changed.
- Why edge distrust increased.
- Why macro risk reduced confidence.

## Required Recommendations

- Reduce simulated exposure.
- Wait for replay recovery.
- Require additional evidence.
- Avoid unstable edge.
- Reduce confidence.
- Avoid concentrated portfolio risk.
- Wait for macro stabilization.
- Maintain paper-only mode.
