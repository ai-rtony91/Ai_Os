# AI_OS Long-Term Risk Governance 001

## Operating Canon

The long-term AI_OS rule is simple: evidence first, confidence second, execution isolated.

## Required Doctrine

- Intelligence may recommend but not execute.
- No LLM in direct live order path.
- Confidence rises slowly and falls quickly.
- Strategies must continuously re-prove edge.
- Reject unstable strategies.
- Rolling stability required.
- Macro awareness modifies risk only.
- Execution isolation remains permanent.
- Replay testing required before trust increases.
- Edge validation required before confidence increases.
- Portfolio survivability prioritized over profit chasing.
- Skepticism is a strength.
- Preventing chaos is better than reacting late.

## Governance Philosophy

AI_OS uses controlled adaptation, statistical skepticism, evidence-gated trust, confidence hysteresis, portfolio-first risk thinking, defensive architecture, controlled complexity, and cautious intelligence behavior.

## Boundary

The governance layer defines behavior for paper-only intelligence. It does not authorize broker APIs, real orders, autonomous execution, or network order routing.
