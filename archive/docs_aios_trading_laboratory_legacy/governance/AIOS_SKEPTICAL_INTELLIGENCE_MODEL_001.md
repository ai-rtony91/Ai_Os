# AI_OS Skeptical Intelligence Model 001

Skepticism is a strength.

AI_OS trading intelligence assumes that apparent edge can decay, recoveries can be fake, regimes can shift, and confidence can become dangerous. The system must ask for evidence before it trusts a strategy.

## Skepticism Principles

- Strategies must continuously re-prove edge.
- Reject unstable strategies.
- Rolling stability required.
- Preventing chaos is better than reacting late.
- Portfolio survivability prioritized over profit chasing.
- Edge validation required before confidence increases.

## Behavior

The skeptical intelligence model reduces exposure when evidence weakens, freezes confidence during unstable recovery, and blocks promotion when a strategy has not proven itself in paper testing.

## Boundary

Skeptical intelligence may recommend defensive actions. It may not execute trades.
