# AI_OS Risk Philosophy 001

AI_OS trading intelligence is built around survivability first. A system that survives can keep learning. A system that chases profit without evidence eventually fails.

## Operating Philosophy

- Survivability first.
- Controlled adaptation.
- Statistical skepticism.
- Evidence-gated trust.
- Confidence hysteresis.
- Portfolio-first risk thinking.
- Defensive architecture.
- Controlled complexity.
- Cautious intelligence behavior.

## Risk View

Risk is not only loss size. Risk includes bad data, unstable regimes, weak edge, fake recovery, overconfidence, portfolio overload, macro instability, execution leakage, and late reaction to chaos.

## Paper-Only Boundary

The risk philosophy applies to paper-only intelligence. It does not authorize live execution, broker APIs, network order routing, autonomous execution, or real orders.
