# AI_OS Operator Explainability Report 001

## Summary

Stage 16.1 makes trading intelligence readable to the operator. It explains why AI_OS trusts or distrusts paper-only conditions.

## Required Explanations

- Why confidence changed.
- Why confidence froze.
- Why ranking dropped.

- Why WATCH activated.

## Governance Philosophy

Explainability before autonomy. Visible risk escalation, visible confidence degradation, and operator-readable survivability reduce hidden chaos. Skepticism must remain visible.

## Safety

This layer is paper-only visualization data. It does not execute, connect to brokers, route network orders, install dependencies, or store secrets.
