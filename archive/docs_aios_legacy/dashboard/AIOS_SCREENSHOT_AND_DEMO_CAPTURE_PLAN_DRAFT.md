# AI_OS Screenshot And Demo Capture Plan Draft

## Purpose

This draft defines future screenshot and demo capture planning for AI_OS operator training. It is DRY_RUN-only and does not capture screenshots, launch apps, or create dashboard outputs.

Dashboard production outputs require separate approval.

## Future Screenshot Workflow

Future screenshot workflow may capture static mock states only after approval, using fixture-only data and safe redaction checks.

## Static Mock Capture

Static mock capture may be used to document cockpit regions, widget layout, alert hierarchy, and operator onboarding examples.

## Tutorial Overlays

Tutorial overlays may highlight panel purpose, validator state, protected-file warnings, and next safe action during training.

## Future Demo Video Concepts

Demo videos may show fixture replay, alert simulation, dashboard navigation, and explanation prompts. They must not show secrets, credentials, broker tokens, private keys, recovery keys, or live trading data.

## Safe Redaction Concepts

Safe redaction should remove paths, usernames, secrets, credentials, broker details, account identifiers, and uncontrolled screen contents when needed.

## Fixture-Only Demo Philosophy

Demos should use fixture-only data. Demo content must not depend on live telemetry, report writing, broker systems, or credentials.

## Operator Training Clips

Operator training clips may explain how to read status, interpret alerts, inspect validators, and confirm protected-file safety.

## Future Stage 44

Future Stage 44 may define demo capture fixtures. No screenshot or demo artifact is created here.
