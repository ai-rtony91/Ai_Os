# AIOS Forex Overnight Profit Execution Readiness V1

## Objective

AIOS is being built toward persistent overnight and multi-day supervised Forex execution. Profitability is a real objective, but not a promise.

The 100 percent to 120 percent return target is aspirational only. It requires evidence, risk-adjusted controls, drawdown limits, sample sufficiency, and Human Owner approval before it can guide live-money escalation.

## Required Evidence Gates

Before overnight or multi-day execution can be armed, AIOS needs proof for:

- positive expectancy;
- controlled drawdown;
- acceptable profit factor;
- sufficient sample size;
- stop-loss policy;
- position sizing;
- exposure cap;
- spread and rollover awareness;
- weekend and market-close behavior;
- disconnect recovery;
- kill switch behavior;
- audit logging;
- owner approval.

## Compounding Gate

Compounding requires realized-profit accounting, maximum-risk caps, drawdown controls, and a rule that prevents adding size from unrealized or unproven gains.

## Withdrawal Gate

Withdrawals require realized profit, a reserve rule, ledger note, and owner approval. Withdrawal logic must not weaken risk controls or hide drawdown.

## Live-Money Gate

Live-money execution requires signed owner approval after demo evidence. Secure persistence, read-only broker proof, risk gates, kill switch proof, audit logs, and a live exception record must all be complete before any live route can arm.
