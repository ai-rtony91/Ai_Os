## Preflight Evidence Behavior

The bundle accepts injected dictionaries only and returns:

- `preflight_schema`
- status and readiness fields
- blockers
- component status
- sanitized evidence
- mobile summary
- safety summary
- next safe action
- protected action status
- execution flags forced false
- persistence flags forced false

Even when ready, `execution_allowed` remains false.

## Account Risk Envelope Behavior

`validate_account_risk_envelope` requires currency, positive balance/equity, non-negative margin and open risk, clear max-loss and daily-stop gates, disabled kill switch, clear portfolio exposure, positive micro risk cap, no account id present, and no account id persistence.

## Instrument Envelope Behavior

`validate_instrument_envelope` requires an OANDA-style instrument symbol, enabled trading, open market, supported instrument, valid min/max unit bounds, requested units within the instrument bounds, and requested units no greater than 1000.

## Quote/Spread Envelope Behavior

`validate_quote_spread_envelope` requires positive bid/ask, ask greater than bid, positive spread, spread within max spread, fresh quote, non-negative quote age within max age, verified market data source, and no broker call.

## Order Intent Envelope Behavior

`validate_live_order_intent_envelope` requires instrument, BUY or SELL side, units greater than 0 and no more than 1000, stop loss, take profit, risk/reward ratio of at least 1, confirmed risk cap, confirmed stop loss, confirmed take profit, one-trade-only, micro-size-only, no order executed, and no broker call.

## Integration With Final Bridge, Credential Injection, Connector, And Transport

The bundle imports and uses readiness constants from:

- `final_live_operator_bridge_v1`
- `protected_runtime_credential_injection_v1`
- `oanda_live_runtime_connector_v2`
- `oanda_live_http_transport_v1`

The existing bridge, injection harness, connector, transport, and executor modules were not edited.

## Mobile Operator Summary Behavior

The mobile summary exposes compact truth fields for mode, status, blockers, next safe action, instrument, side, units, stop loss, take profit, max loss gate, daily stop gate, kill switch, spread, and quote freshness. It is display-only and keeps execution disabled.
